Stable Protection Hook Live
A Uniswap v4 hook that dynamically adjusts swap fees across five depeg zones to protect stablecoin LPs from adverse selection. Fees scale from 0.05% in healthy conditions to 1% during severe depeg, with a circuit breaker for extreme events.
0.05%→1% fee curve · 5 depeg zones · Audited by Spearbit
Dynamic Fee Hook Live
A Uniswap v4 hook that reads live Chainlink oracle data to set fees across five volatility zones. Protects LPs from toxic flow during high-volatility windows while staying competitive when markets are calm — targeting ETH/USDC and cbBTC/USDC pools.
Chainlink-driven · 5 vol zones · ETH/USDC · cbBTC/USDC
RWAGate Hook Live
A compliance-native gatekeeper hook that enforces KYC whitelist access for Real World Asset token pools. Every swap and liquidity action is validated against an on-chain registry with per-address expiry — making compliant RWA trading possible on Uniswap v4.
KYC-gated · On-chain registry · Per-address expiry
Async Limit Order Hook Live
A Uniswap v4 hook that turns concentrated single-tick liquidity into trustless on-chain limit orders. Orders fulfil and redeem automatically when the market crosses the target price — no keepers, no off-chain infrastructure required.
Keeper-less · Trustless fills · Single-tick liquidity

Frequently Asked Questions

Mantua.AI is an agent-driven liquidity protocol for stablecoins that allows users and institutions to manage stablecoin positions, deploy liquidity, and execute automated rebalancing strategies through natural language. It combines Uniswap v4 hooks, autonomous AI agents, and real-time onchain execution to transform user intent into automated liquidity actions. The result is a programmable liquidity layer optimized for stablecoins, RWAs, and yield-bearing dollar assets.
Managing stablecoin liquidity today is operationally manual, interface-fragmented, strategy-dependent, and exposed to peg risk across pools, venues, and market conditions. Mantua.AI enables liquidity providers, stablecoin issuers, fintech platforms, and RWA protocols to deploy peg-aware liquidity, automated rebalancing strategies, and yield-seeking routing logic directly from natural-language instructions, executed onchain via agent-managed Uniswap v4 hook strategies.
The Stable Protection Hook is a Uniswap v4 hook that dynamically adjusts swap fees based on how far a stablecoin pair has drifted from its peg. It defines five depeg zones — Healthy, Minor, Moderate, Severe, and Critical — each with progressively higher fees to discourage arbitrage during volatility and protect LPs from adverse selection. When the peg is healthy, fees stay low (0.05%). As deviation increases, fees scale up automatically to as high as 1%, and a circuit breaker can pause swaps entirely during extreme depeg events.
